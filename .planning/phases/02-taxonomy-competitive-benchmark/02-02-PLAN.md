**Section 7: Asset Universe Mapping** (numbered 7 to follow Section 6 Trigger Taxonomy)

**7.1 Asset Classification Tiers**

Define 4 asset classification tiers based on market cap, liquidity, and risk profile:

| Tier | Name | Examples | Criteria | Default Notification Behavior |
|------|------|----------|----------|-------------------------------|
| T1 | Major | BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, XRP, ADA | Top 10 by market cap on Bit2Me, daily volume > EUR 100K | All trigger families eligible. Market triggers fire at default thresholds. |
| T2 | Stablecoin | USDT, USDC, DAI, EUROC | Pegged to fiat currency | Cross-sell (Earn) eligible. Volatility triggers suppressed (intentionally stable). De-peg alert at >2% deviation as Risk/Protective (Family F). |
| T3 | Mid-cap / Long-tail | DOT, LINK, AVAX, ATOM, and ~100-200 others | Listed on Bit2Me, daily volume EUR 1K-100K | User-configured alerts eligible. Market triggers only if volume > EUR 10K/day. |
| T4 | Micro-cap / Memecoin | PEPE, BONK, FLOKI, and ~200+ others | Listed on Bit2Me, daily volume < EUR 1K | User-configured alerts only. NO proactive market triggers (too noisy). New listing alerts eligible. |

**7.2 Product-Asset Eligibility Matrix**

Comprehensive table mapping each Bit2Me product to its eligible asset subset. Include all 11 products from PROJECT.md: Wallet, Brokerage, Pro, Earn, Card, Loan, Launchpad, Space Center, Pay, Wealth, API. For each: asset scope description, approximate count, and notes.

Note: Use dynamic references ("Earn-eligible assets") rather than hard-coded lists. Implementation should use a BigQuery view (bit2me_lifecycle.asset_product_eligibility).

**7.3 Trigger-Family Asset Scope Rules**

Table mapping each of the 6 trigger families (A-F) to their asset scope rule, using the Layer 1/2/3 framework from 02-RESEARCH.md:
- Family A: All listed assets (Layer 1)
- Family B: Top 50-100 by volume (T1 + top T3), EUR 10K/day minimum, stablecoins excluded from volatility
- Family C: Assets the user has interacted with
- Family D: Not asset-specific (lifecycle stage-based)
- Family E: Product-specific subsets (Earn-eligible, Loan-eligible, Pro-eligible)
- Family F: Assets the user currently holds or has collateralized

**7.4 Asset Scope Governance**

Rules for maintaining the asset universe over time:
- Dynamic vs. static: BigQuery view, not hard-coded lists
- Volume threshold maintenance: quarterly review
- Stablecoin de-peg monitoring: >2% deviation for >1 hour triggers Family F alert
- New listing process: auto-enters Wallet/Brokerage, manual review for other products
