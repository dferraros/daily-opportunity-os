## 4. BUILD vs BUY DECISION

### Option A: Build Internally
- **Cost Year 1**: €65.5K (€40K SEO + €45K Dev + €25K Content + €5K tools + €24K content production)
- **Timeline**: 6-12 months to MVP
- **Risks**: Expertise gaps (ML/NLP), curving learning, opportunity cost
- **Expertise Gap**: Spain doesn't have strong pSEO specialists available

### Option B: Buy (Consultant Pilot)
- **Cost Year 1**: €37K (€9K pilot + €3K maintenance + €25K Scale Piece 2)
- **Timeline**: 4 weeks to MVP
- **Risks**: Moderate (consultant dependency, but with knowledge transfer)
- **Advantages**: Proven system, rapid execution, reusable architecture

### Recommendation: HYBRID
1. **Months 1-4**: Consultants (€9K) → MVP + documentation + knowledge transfer
2. **Months 5-12**: Hybrid → Consultants (€25K) + Bit2Me internal team on scaling
3. **Year 2+**: Bit2Me internal → Use templates + documented playbook

**Savings vs build**: €28.5K+ in Year 1 (3.8× cheaper)

## 5. STRATEGIC ADVANTAGES OF THIS MOMENT

### Regulatory Position
Bit2Me is the **FIRST Spanish exchange with MiCA license**. This regulatory authority can become an E-E-A-T advantage in SEO (especially vs Binance which faces regulatory questions).

### AI Search Emergence (GEO - Generative Engine Optimization)
**Critical Finding**: ChatGPT cites Bit2Me with **74% positive sentiment** (highest among analyzed exchanges).

**Problem**: ChatGPT mentions Bit2Me initially but then redirects to Binance/Kraken for "trading" and "fees". Bit2Me positioned as "beginner/conservative" not "home".

**Opportunity**: Optimize content for "crypto treasury for SMEs", "long-term savings", "compliance" → shift narrative in AI. This is what consultant proposes in GEO optimization.

### Geographic Efficiency
Bit2Me already gets 27.8% traffic from Spain (core market) + 17.7% from Portugal + 19.2% from Italy. This is GOOD geographic distribution. Not scattered (unlike earlier Semrush data showed Finland at 31%). Ready to scale with targeted pSEO.

### Non-Negotiable Conditions (for €9K approval)

1. **Knowledge Transfer**
   - 100% documentation of all templates, prompts, processes
   - 2-day training for Bit2Me team

   - Bit2Me owns 100% of generated content

3. **Scalability Clarity**
   - Documented path from €9K (4 weeks) → €25K (8 weeks)

4. **Go/No-Go Metrics**
