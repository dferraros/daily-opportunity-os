## Part 1: The Coinbase Playbook (27M Monthly Visitors)

### What They Do

Coinbase receives **26.9 million organic visitors per month** (March 2023 data), accounting for 44.9% of total traffic.

**The Secret:** 79.5% of their organic traffic comes from just THREE programmatic page types:

| Page Type | Monthly Visitors | % of Total | Example URL |
|-----------|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| Converter Pages | 8.5M | 31.6% | /converter/btc/eur |
| Price Pages | 7.2M | 26.8% | /price/bitcoin |
| "How to Buy" Pages | 5.7M | 21.2% | /how-to-buy/ethereum |
| **TOTAL** | **21.4M** | **79.5%** | |

Source: GuerrillaBuzz analysis via Ahrefs, March 2023

### Key Stats

- **800,000+ keywords** ranked
- **37,000+ commercial keywords** on page 1
- **72% of organic traffic** from programmatic SEO
- **2.4 million monthly visits** from converter pages alone (achieved in just 5 months after launch in January 2022)
- **20+ country subfolders** with translated content

### Page Structure That Works

Every Coinbase price page includes:
1. **Real-time price chart** + "Buy [Coin]" CTA
2. **About section** (~670 words, SEO-optimized)
3. **Related assets** (reduces bounce rate)
4. **FAQ section** (structured data for featured snippets)
5. **Internal links** to blog and other coins

### Speed to Results

> "Coinbase began publishing its converter pSEO pages in January 2022. Within 5 months, these pages generated more than 2.4 million organic monthly visits."
> — Jaume Ros, SEO Expert

## Part 3: Real Case Study — 6x Traffic in 9 Months

### The Changelly Case (Crypto Exchange)

**Starting Point (August 2021):**
- 89,340 monthly organic users
- Strong domain authority
- 1M+ pages (but poorly optimized)
- 35,000+ referring domains

**Problems Identified:**
- Duplicate content across language versions
- Missing canonical tags
- Weak internal linking
- Only ~60 optimized commercial pages
- German/Spanish versions used English content

**Solution Implemented:**
- Technical SEO fixes (crawl efficiency, Core Web Vitals)
- Content localization (EN, DE, ES)
- 60+ optimized commercial pages created
- 28 blog posts per month
- 12-17 backlinks per language monthly

## Part 4: The GEO Revolution (AI Search)

### Why This Matters NOW

| Platform | Usage | Status |
|----------|-------|--------|
| ChatGPT | 200M+ weekly users, 2.5B daily prompts | 5th most visited website globally |
| Perplexity | 500M+ queries/month | Fastest growing AI search |
| Google AI Overviews | 50%+ of searches | Now default experience |

Source: Multiple industry reports, 2025

### Traffic Shift Data

- **AI-referred sessions grew 527%** between January-May 2025
- **Traffic from AI sources surged 1,300%** during 2024 holiday season
- **53% of consumers** plan to use AI for shopping in 2025
- **Gartner prediction**: Traditional search drops 25% by 2026, 50% by 2028

### How GEO Differs from SEO

| Traditional SEO | GEO (AI Search) |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| Rank on page 1 | Get cited in AI answers |
| Drive clicks | Earn mentions |
| Optimize for keywords | Optimize for questions |
| Build backlinks | Build citations |
| Traffic to website | Brand in AI responses |

### What Gets Cited by AI (Research Data)

**ChatGPT citation sources:**
- Wikipedia: 47.9% of factual queries
- High-ranking list articles
- Established directories
- News sources with awards/accreditation

**Perplexity citation sources:**
- Reddit: 46.7% of top sources
- Fresh content (published within 90 days)
- Real-time web results

**Critical Finding:** Only 11% citation overlap between ChatGPT and Perplexity—meaning you need **platform-specific strategies**.

### GEO Strategy That Works

1. **Get listed in top-ranking comparison articles** (most important factor)
2. **Appear in authoritative directories** (Wikipedia, industry databases)
3. **Publicize awards and certifications** (MiCA license, Bank of Spain)
4. **Structure content for AI parsing** (clear definitions, FAQ schema)
5. **Build brand mentions** across trusted sources

**Bit2Me Advantage:** First MiCA license + Bank of Spain registration = strong authority signals for AI.
