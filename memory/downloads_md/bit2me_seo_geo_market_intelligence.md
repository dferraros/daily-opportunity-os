## 2.3 Competitor SEO Strategies Deep Dive

### Binance: The Scale Machine

**Traffic Generators:**
- **Prices section:** 3M visitors/month from "[Coin] price" queries
- **Academy:** 887,900 monthly visitors, 8+ content clusters
- **"How to buy [Coin]":** Less competitive than "Buy [Coin]", same volume
- **International:** 13+ language hubs with localized content

**Key Tactic:** They changed URL structure from `/buy-algorand` to `/how-to-buy-algorand` because:
- "Buy Algorand" → KD 37 (competitive), 1,100 searches/month
- "How to buy Algorand" → KD lower, same volume, higher intent match

### Coinbase: The Programmatic SEO King

**Traffic Generators:**
- **Price pages:** "Shiba Inu price" → 10,000 visits/page
- **Converter pages:** "Convert BTC to SOL" → millions of combinations
- **How-to content:** 11,500+ keywords with "how" in search term

**Key Tactic:** Programmatic SEO (pSEO)
- Built template-based pages that auto-populate
- Converter pages launched Jan 2022 → 2.4M visits by May 2022
- Each page includes: Price widget, CTA, FAQ, related content

**The Math:**
```
Coinbase has 37,000+ commercial/transactional keywords on page 1
If each averages just 100 visits/month = 3.7M visits
At 2% conversion = 74,000 new users/month from SEO alone
```

### Kraken: The Compliance Play

**Strategy:**
- Strong Spanish language support
- Registered with Bank of Spain
- 400+ cryptocurrencies
- Focus on trust signals over volume

**Relevance to Bit2Me:** Kraken proves you can compete on trust/compliance, not just volume.
