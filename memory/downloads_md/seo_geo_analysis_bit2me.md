## 1. Market Context: Why SEO/GEO Matters Now

### The Competitive Landscape

| Exchange | Monthly Organic Traffic | SEO Strategy |
|----------|------------------------|--------------|
| **Binance** | 13M+ visitors/month | Programmatic SEO, 13+ languages, "Buy [Coin]" & "How to buy [Coin]" pages |
| **Coinbase** | 15.5M visitors/month | 37,000+ first-page keywords, converter pages (2.4M visits in 5 months) |
| **Kraken** | ~2M visitors/month | Educational content, localized pages |
| **Bit2Me** | ~500K-1M (estimated) | Academy content, Spain-focused, limited programmatic SEO |

**Key Insight:** Coinbase's programmatic "converter" pages generated **2.4 million monthly visits within 5 months** of launch. This is exactly what the proposal aims to replicate for Bit2Me.

### The GEO Revolution (Critical for 2026)

Traditional search is declining. The data is clear:

- **ChatGPT:** 200M+ weekly active users
- **Perplexity:** 100M+ queries/month
- **Google AI Overviews:** Now appear on 50%+ of searches
- **Gartner Prediction:** Traditional search volume drops 25% by 2026, 50% by 2028

**What this means for Bit2Me:**
- Users increasingly ask "¿Cuál es el mejor exchange para comprar Bitcoin en España?" to ChatGPT, not Google
- If Bit2Me isn't optimized for AI citation, competitors will own that answer
- GEO is not optional—it's survival

## 2. Reverse Engineering: What Top Exchanges Do

### Binance's Playbook
1. **URL Structure Optimization:** Changed from `/buy-algorand` to `/how-to-buy-algorand` (less competitive, same volume)
2. **Prices Section:** 3M visitors/month from "[Coin] price" queries
3. **Binance Academy:** 887,900 monthly visitors, highly diversified content
4. **International SEO:** 13+ languages, localized content hubs

### Coinbase's Playbook
1. **Programmatic SEO (pSEO):** Thousands of auto-generated pages
   - Price pages: "Shiba Inu price" → 10,000 monthly visits per page
   - Converter pages: "Convert BTC to SOL" → millions of combinations
2. **High-Intent Focus:** 37,000+ commercial/transactional keywords on page 1
3. **FAQ Schema:** Structured data for featured snippets
4. **20+ Country Subfolders:** Localized, translated content at scale

### What Bit2Me Does Today
- ✅ Bit2Me Academy (strong educational content)
- ✅ Buy pages (comprar bitcoin, comprar ethereum)
- ✅ Spanish market leadership
- ❌ Limited programmatic SEO
- ❌ No systematic intent-based landing pages
- ❌ No GEO optimization strategy
- ❌ Portugal market underdeveloped

## 5. GEO: The Bigger Opportunity

The proposal mentions "SEO/GEO" but focuses mostly on SEO. Here's why GEO deserves more attention:

### Current State of AI Search for Crypto

I tested: *"¿Cuál es el mejor exchange para comprar Bitcoin en España?"* on ChatGPT/Perplexity.

**Typical AI response pattern:**
1. Mentions Binance (global leader)
2. Mentions Coinbase (US leader, known brand)
3. May mention local options
4. Criteria: regulation, fees, ease of use

**Where Bit2Me should win:**
- ✅ Spanish company, Bank of Spain registered
- ✅ Spanish language support
- ✅ EU/MiCA compliance
- ✅ €150M insurance

**But AI needs structured data to cite Bit2Me:**
- Schema markup for organization, products, reviews
- Clear E-E-A-T signals (authorship, credentials)
- Structured FAQ content
- Presence in authoritative sources (Wikipedia, news)

### GEO Investment Priorities

| Priority | Action | Investment |
|----------|--------|------------|
| 1 | Schema markup implementation | €1,000-2,000 |
| 2 | Wikipedia presence | Internal effort |
| 3 | Structured content templates | Part of proposal |
| 4 | AI crawler access (GPTBot, PerplexityBot) | Technical config |
| 5 | GEO monitoring tools | €89-500/month |
