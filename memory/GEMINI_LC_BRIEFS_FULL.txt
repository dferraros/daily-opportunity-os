Spain Lifecycle Population — Feb 2026
1. Feb 13–15 Round — What Happened
Key learning: Dormant users need a non-transactional first touch. Pushing “Buy” to someone who hasn’t traded in 90–180 days is too much friction. Show them what they’re missing (portfolio performance, Earn yield) before asking for a trade.
2. Strategic Focus — Why These 3 Populations
Revenue is down. Patri confirmed the trend on Feb 15. The fastest recovery path does not come from acquiring new users — it comes from converting users who are already on the platform with money and intent. The three populations below are ranked by expected revenue velocity.

Population 1 — L3 Near-Dormant (Priority: HIGH)
Population 2 — L1 Deposited-Only (Priority: HIGH)
Population 3 — L4 Early Dormant (Priority: MEDIUM)
4. UTM Attribution Setup
Every CleverTap campaign must have UTM parameters defined BEFORE sending. Without this, Qlik Transaction Attribution cannot link revenue to a specific campaign. This was the issue in the Feb 13 round.
5. Sample Size Reference
Minimum users per variant needed to detect a real effect with 80% statistical power (α=0.05, two-sided). Do not run A/B tests below these thresholds.
