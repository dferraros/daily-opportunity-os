10. KEY FORMULAS & DEFINITIONS
================================================================================

FM (First Monetization):
  = MIN(event_timestamp) WHERE revenue_amount > €threshold
  Product-specific thresholds: Brokerage €50, Earn €5, Card €1

ROAS (Return on Ad Spend):
  = Revenue / Spend
  North Star for paid channels

mROAS (Marginal ROAS):
  = ΔRevenue / ΔSpend
  Decision threshold for scaling: if mROAS < 1 → STOP

Revenue LC (Lifecycle):
  = Revenue within 12 months of FM
  96% of total Bit2Me revenue comes from LC

CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):
  = Spend / New FMs
  Payback threshold: <12 months

Viral Coefficient K:
  = (Invitations sent × Conv rate) / Inviter
  K > 1 = viral, K < 1 = unsustainable

Health Score:
  = (-1 * last_login_days_ago + last_action_balance + product_diversity + btc_condition)
  Threshold: < -1 = AT_RISK cohort
