## What This Is

Bit2Me's full go-to-market playbook for Paris Blockchain Week 2026. Covers booth presence, pre-event acquisition campaigns, landing pages, PR/comms, and post-event lead nurture. A triple-goal play: user acquisition, brand positioning, and B2B/partnership development — executed in a 2-4 week sprint.
