## 1. Offer Name (Internal Reference)

PBW-60-ZERO — Paris Blockchain Week 60-Day Zero Fee Offer

## 2. Offer Mechanics

- **What:** 60 calendar days of zero trading fees on all spot trades
- **Eligibility:** New accounts only, registered via the dedicated PBW landing page (bit2me.com/pbw)
- **Trigger:** Account created between April 1, 2026 and April 30, 2026 (registration window)
- **Fee waiver period:** 60 calendar days from account creation date
- **Cap:** None — all spot trades within the 60-day window are zero-fee
- **Exclusions:** Does not apply to OTC, institutional API trades (separate pricing), or any non-spot product
- **Redemption:** Automatic — fee waiver applied at account level upon registration via /pbw URL. No promo code required.

## 3. MiCA Compliance Statement

This offer is a fee waiver (fee suppression), NOT a cash bonus, token distribution, or yield promotion.

- **MiCA Article 76:** Permits promotional fee waivers as standard commercial practice. Fee suppression does not constitute a financial inducement under MiCA definitions.
- **MiCA Article 77:** Bonus promotion rules (which require enhanced disclosure and potential cool-off periods) are NOT triggered because no monetary value is transferred to the user. The user simply pays zero fees — Bit2Me absorbs the cost.
- **No APY or yield claims** are made in any asset.
- **No guaranteed returns** language appears anywhere.
- **No token or cash distribution** occurs.

Diego: This structure has been pre-validated against MiCA Article 76-77 marketing requirements. If you see a compliance gap, please flag it in the Approval Log (Section 9 of the review package).

## 5. Prohibited Framing

Do NOT use in any copy:
- "Bonus" or "reward" (triggers MiCA Article 77)
- "Guaranteed" anything
- "Investment opportunity" or "returns"
- "Limited time only" without specifying the actual dates
- "Free money" or "free crypto"
- Comparative claims without data ("better than Coinbase")
- Any language implying regulator endorsement ("approved by CNMV for you")
