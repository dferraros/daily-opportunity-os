## Section 4: Evaluation Criteria

Rate each discovered event on these 4 factors (1–5 scale). Minimum threshold to RSVP: average score >= 3.

| Factor | Weight | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|--------|--------|---|---|---|
| Audience overlap with Bit2Me B2B targets (banks, fintechs, asset managers, custody) | Core | No overlap | Some overlap | Direct overlap (compliance, custody, institutional crypto) |
| Institutional density (C-suite vs general attendees) | Core | Mostly general public | Mixed | C-suite / partner-level only |
| Networking format (structured intros vs open bar vs panel-only) | Secondary | Panel-only, no networking | Mix of panel + networking | Structured intros, roundtable, hosted dinner |
| Schedule fit (does it conflict with booth hours or pre-booked B2B meetings?) | Hard filter | Major conflict | Minor overlap, manageable | No conflict |

**Priority assignment:**
- Average score >= 4 → **HIGH** — RSVP immediately
- Average score 3–3.9 → **MEDIUM** — RSVP if capacity allows
- Average score < 3 → **LOW** — skip unless serendipitous

## Section 5: RSVP Protocol

Follow these steps for every event Daniel decides to attend:

1. **RSVP on Luma** (or request invite for invite-only events): Submit the form. For invite-only events, look for the host's LinkedIn or Twitter to send a direct request with context.
2. **Add to calendar** with exact location (full Paris address), start time, and end time. Block 30 minutes travel buffer before and after.
3. **Prepare 2-sentence intro tailored to audience:**
   - General version: "Daniel Ferraro, Head of Growth at Bit2Me — Spain's first MiCA-authorized exchange. We're building regulated infrastructure for European banks and we're at PBW with a booth at Carrousel du Louvre."
   - Compliance event version: "Daniel Ferraro, Head of Growth at Bit2Me. We hold Spain's CNMV CASP authorization under MiCA and we process seized crypto assets for Europol — happy to talk regulatory positioning."
   - Investor event version: "Daniel Ferraro, Head of Growth at Bit2Me. We're backed by Bankinter, BBVA, and Tether, and B2B is now 27% of our revenue — would love to share what's working."
4. **Bring NFC card** (Popl or Wave Connect) for frictionless contact exchange. Confirm card is loaded with updated LinkedIn + bit2me.com/pbw link.
5. **After each event:** Add new contacts to B2B Google Sheet within 24 hours. Field: `source = LUMA_[event_name]`. Add a "temperature" (H/M/C) and one-line next action.
