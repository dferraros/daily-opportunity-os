Mental Accounting: EUR [X] parado = EUR [Y] perdido al ano en rendimiento potencial.
Benchmark: Coinbase staking adoption post-email: +23% activation when personalized with idle balance amount.
Bit2Me Earn: 14-15% APY (benchmark: superior a Coinbase 3-5% staking).

Trigger entry: balance_idle_days >= 7 AND no Earn product active
Hub interaction: Hub pausa, spoke se activa. Si usuario no tiene Hub activo, spoke se activa igualmente.
Stop condition: Earn activation confirmed OR 14 dias sin conversion.
