- Peak trading time Spain/Europe: 21:00h benchmark — validate

- Product graduation cadence: savings → premium → crypto
- Behavioral trigger timing (post-success vs time-based)
- Cross-sell CTR data

**N26:**

- Timing from first purchase
- Copy angle (emotional vs rational)
- Lift data (CTR, conversion, retention)
